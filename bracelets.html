GSA Bracelet Making!



The Basics

Before you start with any crazy designs, it's important to learn the basic steps!

Preparing

First make sure you have your materials!

Choose the colors that you want your bracelet to be (usually 2-5 colors depending on the bracelet) and measure out about 20 inches of each color string.

Then bundle the strings all together at one point and tie a knot at the top to hold them together.

Then tape at the top on a flat surface to hold everything in place.

Forwards knot

Step 1

To make a forwards knot, take the strand farthest to the left and cross it horizontally on top making a "4" shape

Step 2

Pull the strand that you made the 4 shape with under the next leftmost strand

Step 3

Hold onto the strand that you crossed under with your left hand and the strand that got crossed under with your right. Pull to the left with your left hand so the strand goes all the way to the top where the knot is. That strand should now be one string to the right

Backwards knot

Step 1

To make a backwards knot, take the strand farthest to the right and cross it horizontally on top making a backwards "4" shape

Step 2

Pull the strand that you made the backwards 4 shape with under the next rightmost strand

Step 3

Hold onto the strand that you crossed under with your right hand and the strand that got crossed under with your left. Pull to the right with your right hand so the strand goes all the way to the top where the knot is. That strand should now be one string to the left
